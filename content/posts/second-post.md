Hugo is a fantastic static site generator. It's fast, simple, and perfect for blogs and documentation.

## Why Hugo?
- Blazing fast build times
- Simple Markdown content
- Great theming system
- No database required

Perfect for the ngodingkuy philosophy of learning, building, and shipping!
